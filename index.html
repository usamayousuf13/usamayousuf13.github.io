ABOUT US

If there is a uniting force to bring people closer together, then it is their shared love and passion for food. Keeping this ideology in mind, Bestfood.pk was a brainchild of our creators who wanted to provide a platform to the people so they could share this feeling. We Pakistanis, despite our political, social and economical differences are quite the pleasure seekers, especially when it comes to fine dining and gorging on good food.

Bestfood.pk is an idea, which evolved from a fluke and grew out to be a fully-supported and reliable website. It aims to improve the standards and services offered by restaurants, provide healthy competition for newly established eateries, and reassure t he people that the food they order will be worth every penny. It will also act as an educational platform, but for food only; by spreading awareness amongst the masses about the restaurants that are trending, and encouraging them to spend their money wisely on the best food places in Pakistan.
